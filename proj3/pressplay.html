infactitsthewayitis,2024




Combination of technology and art has allowed us to create more interactive and immersive works. In this piece, I have combined my deep appreciation for the traditional painting medium with the possibilities offered by technology. I experimented with CodeArt using P5.js and Blender to generate visuals that were projected onto a self-portrait using Max 8. This piece flows almost as a stream of consciousness, reflecting on my life and the sense of spiritual emptiness and alienation I often feel. This spiritual unfulfillment and alienation can be traced back to my lack of resources, which forces me to "Drone" away at a minimum-wage job as I try my best to find a resolution.
