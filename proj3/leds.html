Leo || LED ARCH Project




This was a intro project into electronics focused on learning the fundacmentals of soldering and electronics to make a arch filled with colorful LEDs that would light up when stepped on a DIY swtich. Nine LEDs were used in parallel series combination, three branches of LEDs with each branch containing three bulbs in series combination. For the presentation of the LEDs I arrenged them in the constellation of Leo.
