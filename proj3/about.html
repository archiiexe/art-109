BIO

Since childhood, archie has always been driven by curiosity. archie noticed things that were often overlooked by most and would devote a lot of time to matters he found interesting. At a young age he took interest in Physics and Electronics. Often tinkering with household electronics and breaking them. Growing up in the cultural hub of Kathmandu, Nepal, surrounded by music and arts, it was meant for him to take up arts sooner or later. However, it was not until his late teens he started his journey in arts instead of science. Dipping his feet in the art world firstly through a camera and then moving on to experiment with different mediums. His special interest lie in being able to incorporate Technology and Electronics into traditional mediums and the Arts in general.
