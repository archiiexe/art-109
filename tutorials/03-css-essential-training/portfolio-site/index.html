since childhood, archie has been guided by curiosity. often noticed details others overlooked and dedicated fully to whatever sparked interest. Early on, drawn to Science and Electronics—frequently dismantling household gadgets in an effort to understand how they worked. growing up in the vibrant cultural landscape of Kathmandu, Nepal, surrounded by music and art, it seemed only a matter of time before being gravitated toward the arts. However, it wasn't until his late teens that he shifted his focus from science to creative expression. the artistic journey began with photography and gradually expanded into various mediums. archie's work is driven by a desire to merge technology and traditional art forms. especially interested in incorporating electronics and digital tools into his creative process, blending technical precision with artistic intuition to create innovative, hybrid works.
